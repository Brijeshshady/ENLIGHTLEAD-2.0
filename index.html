Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our SAP training programs.

Do I need prior technical experience?
While a basic understanding of business processes is helpful, our curriculum is designed to take you from a beginner to an advanced level. We provide foundational modules for those completely new to SAP.
Does Enlightlead offer job placement assistance?
Yes! Our Professional tier includes guaranteed placement assistance. We help with resume building, mock interviews, and connect you with our network of hiring partners in the global tech and consulting industries.
How long does it take to get certified?
Typically, students complete their training and feel ready to sit for their SAP certification exams within 3 to 6 months of active study, depending on the module.
Are the study materials updated for S/4HANA?
Absolutely. All our course materials, sandbox access, and hands-on exercises are built on the latest SAP S/4HANA platform to ensure you learn the most current technology.
